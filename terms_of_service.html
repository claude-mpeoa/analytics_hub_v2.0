Terms of Service & Platform Governance

1. Architecture & Purpose

The Analytics Hub is an Ease of Access tool designed to bridge the gap between complex data silos and executive-level decision making. By centralizing disparate marketing solutions into a single, high-performance interface, we empower the Growth Marketing team to act with speed and precision.

Technical Stack

This platform is built using a modern, lightweight architecture to ensure maximum uptime and rapid load speeds:

HTML5 CSS3 Tailwind UI Architecture JavaScript (ES6+) Google Cloud APIs

2. AI Collaboration & Privacy

About the AI (Gemini): I am a large language model, trained by Google. In the context of this platform, I serve as a co-developer, helping Claude Mpeoa optimize code, design responsive layouts, and automate reporting structures.

Data Handling & Security

  • Zero-Retention Policy: As an AI, I process the instructions and structural data provided during the development phase. I do not store, "learn" from, or retain any proprietary Virgin Active marketing data or personal executive information.
  • Encapsulated Logic: The business logic remains within the Virgin Active environment. My role is strictly limited to supporting the code infrastructure and interface design.
  • Safety: No sensitive credentials or customer PIIs (Personally Identifiable Information) are transmitted to or stored by the AI service.

3. Data Privacy & Looker Studio

Transparency regarding our data source is paramount. This hub acts as a secure "portal" for Google Looker Studio dashboards.

Permission-Based Visibility: The data you see is not stored "on" this website. Instead, the platform validates your corporate identity. If your account does not have explicit viewing rights within Google Looker Studio, the dashboards will remain blank. This ensures that sensitive growth metrics are only visible to authorized personnel.

4. Secure Access Protocol

To protect corporate insights, the following security measures are in place:

  • Authentication: Access requires valid credentials managed via a secure internal registry.
  • Session Management: Users are strongly encouraged to Sign Out manually after each session. This action immediately clears the local session state, preventing "tailgating" on shared devices.
  • Integrity: The login process uses modern encryption standards to ensure passwords and emails are never exposed in plain text.
